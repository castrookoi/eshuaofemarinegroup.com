Navigating Success, Anchoring Trust in Marine Solutions

Eshua Ofe Marine Limited specializes in providing a full spectrum of expert marine solutions tailored to the dynamic needs of the maritime and offshore industries. Our core services include ship brokerage and chartering, offshore support, marine logistics, marine consultancy, and procurement.
With a steadfast commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability, we empower our clients to optimize operational efficiency and enhance reliability in their maritime ventures. About Us

Eshua Ofe Marine Limited (EOML) is an indigenous Nigerian company providing innovative and reliable marine solutions. We specialize in ship brokerage, marine logistics and consultancy, procurement, and offshore support services, delivering excellence with integrity and industry best practices.

Our team of seasoned maritime professionals and engineers combines technical expertise with a commitment to operational efficiency, helping clients navigate the complexities of marine operations.
