Who We Are

Eshua Ofe Marine Limited (EOML) is a fully registered indigenous Nigerian marine logistics and offshore services company, committed to innovative, reliable, and secure solutions in the maritime sector. Anchored in excellence, we prioritize quality service delivery, client satisfaction, and industry best practices. We specialize in Ship Brokerage & Chartering, Marine Logistics and Consultancy, Offshore Support, and Procurement & Logistics—aligning our services with the oil and gas, shipping, and offshore sectors to enable safe and efficient operations while supporting Nigeria’s blue economy. With operational footprints in Port Harcourt, Cross River, and Lagos, we are positioned to serve clients swiftly across the Gulf of Guinea and inland waterways. Our seasoned professionals, maritime experts, engineers, and logistics specialists bring deep industry knowledge, technical skill, and commitment to excellence. Leveraging our fleet, partnerships, and compliance, we deliver scalable, sustainable solutions with innovation, professionalism, and integrity. At EOML, we are more than service providers—we are long-term partners in your offshore success.
