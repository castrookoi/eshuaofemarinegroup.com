Who We Are

Eshua Ofe Marine Limited (EOML) is a fully registered indigenous Nigerian marine logistics and offshore services company, committed to innovative, reliable, and secure solutions in the maritime sector. Anchored in excellence, we prioritize quality service delivery, client satisfaction, and industry best practices. We specialize in Ship Brokerage & Chartering, Marine Logistics and Consultancy, Offshore Support, and Procurement & Logistics—aligning our services with the oil and gas, shipping, and offshore sectors to enable safe and efficient operations while supporting Nigeria’s blue economy. With operational footprints in Port Harcourt, Cross River, and Lagos, we are positioned to serve clients swiftly across the Gulf of Guinea and inland waterways. Our seasoned professionals, maritime experts, engineers, and logistics specialists bring deep industry knowledge, technical skill, and commitment to excellence. Leveraging our fleet, partnerships, and compliance, we deliver scalable, sustainable solutions with innovation, professionalism, and integrity. At EOML, we are more than service providers—we are long-term partners in your offshore success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to consistently deliver cost-effective, safe, and timely marine logistics and offshore support services that exceed client expectations. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism, operational efficiency, and safety, while embracing modern technologies and sustainable practices. Through a client-focused approach, we aim to build long-lasting partnerships, support economic development, and continually adapt to the evolving needs of the marine and offshore industries.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a globally recognized and trusted leader in marine logistics, ship brokerage, and offshore operations, setting the benchmark for excellence, innovation, and integrity in the maritime industry. We envision a future where our name represents reliability, efficiency, and professionalism, not just within Nigeria, but across international waters—contributing to the growth and transformation of the global maritime sector.

Our Journey

2002 - Our Foundation

Eshua Ofe Marine Limited began operations in 2002, driven by a vision to provide reliable marine logistics and offshore support across Nigeria's waterways.

2005-2015: Growth and Capacity

We expanded our operations, built expertise, and earned trust by consistently delivering dependable solutions to clients in the Oil & Gas and Shipping Industries.

Reputation & Resilience

Through continuous improvement, investment in people, and a focus on safety and innovation, we strengthened our presence in the maritime sector.

2023 - Incorporation & Sustainability Focus

In 2023, Eshua Ofe Marine was formally incorporated, opening a new chapter of sustainable growth, global partnerships, and stronger alignment with the needs of our international and local clients.
